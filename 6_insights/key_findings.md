## Overall Churn Overview
- A significant portion of customers have churned, indicating retention challenges.
- Churn rate varies across different subscription types.

## Subscription-Based Insights
- Customers on basic or low-tier subscription plans show higher churn rates.
- Premium subscription users have better retention compared to standard plans.

## Customer Behavior Patterns
- Customers with frequent support calls tend to churn more.
- Late payment behavior is strongly associated with churn.
- Low usage frequency customers are more likely to leave.

## Risk Segmentation
- A distinct group of high-risk customers was identified based on:
  - High number of support calls
  - Frequent payment delays
  - Low product usage

## Business Impact
- High churn directly affects monthly recurring revenue.
- Retaining high-risk customers can significantly improve profitability.
