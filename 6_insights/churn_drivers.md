# Churn Drivers Analysis

## 1. High Support Dependency
- Customers who contacted support frequently showed higher churn probability.
- Indicates dissatisfaction or unresolved issues.

## 2. Payment Delays
- Repeated late payments correlate strongly with customer churn.
- Suggests affordability issues or low product value perception.

## 3. Low Usage Frequency
- Customers with low engagement are more likely to churn.
- Indicates weak product adoption.

## 4. Subscription Type
- Lower-tier plans experience more churn than premium plans.
- Premium customers receive better value or support.

## 5. Combined Risk Factors
- Customers having multiple risk factors (support issues + payment delay + low usage)
  show the highest churn rates.
